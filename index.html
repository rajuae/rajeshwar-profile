Biography

I am Rajeshwar Reddy K., an IT professional with over 8 years of experience in system analysis, business analysis, system administration, and technical support. I currently work as a Business Technical Analyst at the Centre for Good Governance (CGG) in Hyderabad, where I contribute to critical government projects, helping streamline processes and improve service delivery. My expertise lies in requirement gathering, stakeholder coordination, and managing web-based e-Governance solutions, which I’ve applied in various roles throughout my career.

I hold an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Aurobindo Institute of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad, completed in 2017. Prior to that, I earned my MCA from Adarsh Post Graduate College of Computer Sciences, Hyderabad, in 2012. My foundational studies include a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Wanaparthy College of Computers and Management, Hyderabad, completed in 2009. These academic qualifications have provided me with a strong technical foundation in programming, database management, and IT infrastructure.

Throughout my career, I have gained significant experience across various roles, including Business Technical Analyst, System Analyst, and IT Support Engineer. I have worked on key government e-Governance projects like CROP and PQMS, where I manage everything from requirement analysis to stakeholder coordination and integration with external systems. My technical skills include Java, SQL, API integrations, cloud infrastructure management, and system security. Additionally, I am experienced in project management, ISO 9001:2015 compliance, and leading cross-functional teams to deliver successful technology solutions.
